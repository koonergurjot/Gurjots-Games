How to play Snake+

Guide the snake to glowing fruit to grow, score points, and climb the tiers. Avoid walls, obstacles, and your own tail.

  • Move: Arrow keys / WASD or swipe on touch screens.
  • Pause: Press P or use the Pause button. The game auto-pauses when you switch tabs.
  • Restart: Press R or use the Restart control in the HUD.
  • Goals: Chain snacks quickly for combos, toggle wrap and walls, and try the daily seed for a shared challenge.
