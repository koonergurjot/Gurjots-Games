Actions

Use arrow keys or WASD (swipe on touch) to move. Press R to restart, U or Ctrl+Z to undo, Y or Ctrl+Y to redo, H for hints.

How to play

Combine matching tiles to climb toward 2048 and beyond.

  • SlideUse arrow keys, WASD, or swipe to move every tile in one direction.
  • MergeTiles with the same value collide to double and increase your score.
  • PlanChain merges to build streaks and unlock achievements for 1024, 2048, and more.
  • ToolsUndo/redo, hints, and deterministic RNG help you practice tricky openings.
